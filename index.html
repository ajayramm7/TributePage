Missile Man of India • People's President • Visionary

“Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action.”

About Dr. Kalam

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He played a major role in India’s civilian space and military missile programs and was a key figure in Pokhran-II nuclear tests in 1998.

Loved by millions for his humility, wisdom, and dedication to youth, Dr. Kalam remains an inspiration across generations.

Major Awards

  • Padma Bhushan – 1981
  • Padma Vibhushan – 1990
  • Bharat Ratna – 1997
  • Indira Gandhi Award for National Integration – 1997
  • Ramanujan Award – 2000
  • Hoover Medal – 2008
  • Von Braun Award – 2013
